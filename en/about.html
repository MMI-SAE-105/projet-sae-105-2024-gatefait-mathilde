I am Mathilde Gatefait, a first-year student in BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) in Montbéliard.

This website project is themed "Prehistory of the Future". Its goal is to showcase an object rooted in the past and bring it into the future.

Glasses were the object that spoke to me the most because they are constantly evolving. Why couldn't they be both a correction tool and a technological device?

Through this website, you will discover how I brought glasses into a futuristic world and the medium I used to develop this idea.

Glasses could eventually become compatible with artificial intelligence and become our work tools.
