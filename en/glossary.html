Below, you will find the technical terms used in the articles, along with their definitions to help facilitate your understanding.

Bifocal lenses:

Bifocal lenses are lenses with two distinct vision zones. Distance vision is provided in the upper part of the lens, while near vision is available in the lower part.

Progressive lenses:

Progressive lenses are multifocal lenses with three vision zones. They provide distance vision at the top, intermediate vision in the middle, and near vision at the bottom of the lens.

Virtual reality:

A technique that simulates a three-dimensional environment using a computer; the world thus created.

Mixed reality:

Mixed reality is the combination of virtual reality and augmented reality. It merges these two technologies, enabling real and digital objects to interact and create an immersive experience.
