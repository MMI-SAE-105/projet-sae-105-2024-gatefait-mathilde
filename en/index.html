Welcome to my site dedicated to the evolution of glasses, a journey through their fascinating history and the perspectives of their future.

From their beginnings as simple vision correction tools, glasses have evolved into objects of fashion, technology, and health.

This site not only explores the major milestones of this transformation but also recent innovations and future trends. How might glasses further change our lives in the near future?

With the advent of augmented reality, artificial intelligence, and connected devices, the possibilities seem endless.
