In some movies, glasses are often more than just accessories; they become symbols of power, intelligence, and technology.

SYNOPSIS

Released in 2015, Kingsman The Secret Service*, directed by Matthew Vaughn, is an action film that reinvents the spy genre.

The story follows Eggsy, a young man recruited by a British secret organization, "The Kingsman," to become an elite agent.

During his training, he discovers the world of spies, filled with sophisticated gadgets and perilous missions, while trying to save the world from a technological threat.

Matthew Vaughn

THE SIGNIFICANCE OF GLASSES

In this film, glasses are not just accessories but essential tools for the agents.

Equipped with advanced technologies, they allow for remote communication, discreet filming, augmented reality displays, and even data analysis.

They combine classic elegance with modern technology, becoming indispensable gadgets for spies.

They serve as communication tools but also enable holographic meetings.

"Are these the kind of glasses that give you X-ray vision?"

Eggsy

MORAL PRINCIPLE

The glasses in Kingsman can also be seen as a metaphor for the perspective on the world.

The Kingsman are protectors who view things from a different angle, often hidden from public sight, thanks to their sophisticated gadgets and elite training.

The fact that they "see" threats invisible to others echoes their role as spies safeguarding global security.

FUTURE SUGGESTION

The film suggests potential technological innovations applicable to the real world. The Kingsman glasses could inspire advancements in augmented reality, encrypted communications, or facial recognition.

Google Glass and Ray-Ban are already showing the first steps toward this vision, with image capture and information features.

In the future, it could be possible to create even more advanced glasses capable of enhancing our perception of the world and providing real-time information.

Beyond fiction, Kingsman raises the question of the future of glasses as smart devices.

The film shows how this everyday object could evolve into a major technological tool, defining how we see and interact with the world.

They are not just a fashion accessory. They play a crucial role in the plot as sophisticated spy gadgets.
