Glasses in today's world are a fashion accessory, a statement of style, and a means of self-expression.

MUSIC

Thanks to "Style Glasses," a song by the group Do Not Do, released in July 2023, we explore glasses as a symbol of style and personal expression.

Beyond their primary function of vision correction, they now play a role in fashion.

The lyrics of this song highlight that glasses are much more than a simple accessory; they are a key element of fashion that can transform the appearance and attitude of the wearer. The song adopts a playful tone, focusing on the themes of charm and charisma associated with glasses.

THE ARTISTS

Do Not Do is an independent group known for its tracks blending pop, electro, and a touch of social satire.

The group consists of Romy and Iskander, who are not only musicians but also fashion enthusiasts, inspiring them to write "Style Glasses."

Their music aims to capture aspects of everyday life and turn them into fun or meaningful themes.

In this case, they chose to focus on glasses and their impact on culture and style.

“When I wear my glasses, I have style, I lock my dreams in lenses of class”

Style Glasses - Do Not Do

CONTEMPORARY FASHION

The song "Style Glasses" highlights the importance of glasses in contemporary fashion.

Once seen only as tools for improving vision, glasses have now become essential fashion accessories.

The song emphasizes how different types of frames can reflect a person's personality and influence their image.

It suggests that choosing glasses is like choosing an outfit: it expresses a lifestyle and an identity.

Through references to trends and popular styles, the song draws a direct connection between glasses and fashion.

It demonstrates how this accessory can add a touch of sophistication or coolness to any look.

Listening to "Style Glasses," one realizes that glasses are not just functional but also a powerful way to assert personality and show a sense of fashion.
