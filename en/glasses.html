Glasses are an optical instrument that revolutionized the world. In this article, we will explore their origins and their future evolution.

TIMELINE

13th Century

First Glasses

At first, they were simply used to correct vision problems, mainly for monks.

1838

3D Glasses

3D glasses with red and blue filters allow seeing in three dimensions by simulating depth perception.

1968

Virtual Reality Headset

It fully covers the eyes and offers total immersion in digital worlds, perfect for video games, training, or even medicine.

2013-2021

Google Glasses, Ray-Ban Stories

Google Glass was the first connected glasses with a screen and camera, but they were not commercially successful. Ray-Ban Stories can record videos and respond to voice commands while remaining discreet.

Today

Current Correction Glasses

Today, they are also fashion accessories and high-tech items, with the development of bifocal, multifocal, and tinted lenses.

STYLE GLASSES

2050

Mixed Reality Glasses/Helmet

To replace traditional computers, they could project virtual screens into our field of vision.

2070

Connected Lenses

They could be used as micro-screens, sensors, and miniaturized circuits to provide real-time data while being comfortable to wear.

3000

Connected Implant

It could replace everything by correcting vision and displaying information directly in our field of view.
